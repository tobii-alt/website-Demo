Payment Protocols

We offer flexible payment options to suit your needs. Payments can be made via:

  • Credit/Debit Card (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
  • Direct Debit for recurring memberships
  • Cash payments at the front desk
  • Online payments through our secure portal

All payments are processed securely. Membership fees are non-refundable. Please refer to our terms and conditions for more details.

Membership Information

Choose from a variety of membership plans designed to fit your lifestyle and fitness goals:

  • Monthly Membership: $50/month with no long-term commitment.
  • Annual Membership: $500/year with additional perks and discounts.
  • Student Membership: $30/month with a valid student ID.
  • Family Membership: $120/month for up to 4 family members.

Memberships include access to all gym facilities, group classes, and special events. Additional fees may apply for personal training and spa services.
